Day
1

Monday

14 March 2022

  • Reach Dehradun in the morning.
  • Reporting time at ISBT/Prince Chowk Bus stop Dehradun is 7:00 AM
  • Drive to Pantwari village via Mussoorie. The transport halts at Kempty falls for breakfast.
  • Reach Pantwari village via Nainbagh. Get freshened up, have lunch in a hotel and then start the first leg of your trek to Nag Tibba base camp.
  • Upon arrival at Nag Tibba base camp, get served with hot tea and snacks followed by a lip-smacking dinner.
  • Overnight stay in camps surrounded by dwarfed hills and vast valleys with numerous glittering hamlets scattered till far among them.
Day
1

Tuesday

15 March 2022

  • Early morning, wake up to the mesmerizing beauty and fill yourself with breakfast.
  • Start your trek to Nag Tibba top at 3084 ft.
  • Reach Nag Tibba top, and witness the stunning views of the Himalayan peaks like Swarga-Rohini, Bandar Poonch, Kedarnath, and Gangotri.
  • Get served with mouth-watering lunch after the Nagtibba trek.
  • Then start the descend to Pantwari village.
  • End the journey by a drive to Dehradun with a bagful of memories.
  • Drop at Dehradun before 9:30 PM.
  • Take return Volvo from Dehradun.
  • Reach Delhi the next day.
